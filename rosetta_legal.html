Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2025

At Rosetta Project ("we," "us," or "our"), we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our website (rosetta.fun) and software (Rosetta PNO).

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account Information: When you create an account or sign up for updates, we collect your email address and name
  • Payment Information: If you make a donation or pre-order hardware, we collect payment details through our secure payment processor (Stripe). We do not store your full credit card information
  • Communications: When you contact us via email or support channels, we collect the content of your messages

Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: We collect information about how you interact with our software and website, including features used, session duration, and performance metrics
  • Device Information: We collect device type, operating system, browser type, and IP address
  • Cookies: We use essential cookies for site functionality and optional analytics cookies (which you can opt out of)

Software-Specific Data

  • Practice Statistics: Rosetta PNO may collect anonymous practice data (songs played, duration, accuracy) to improve the software. This data is aggregated and cannot identify you personally
  • MIDI Data: We do not collect or store your MIDI input data or musical performances

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve Rosetta PNO and our services
  • Process donations and hardware pre-orders
  • Send you updates, newsletters, and important service announcements (you can opt out anytime)
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve user experience
  • Detect and prevent fraud or security issues
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your data with:

  • Service Providers: Payment processors (Stripe), email services (e.g., ConvertKit), and hosting providers who help us operate our services
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or to protect our rights and safety
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the acquiring entity

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including encryption, secure servers, and access controls. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and personal data
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time
  • Data Portability: Request your data in a portable format

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@rosetta.fun

6. Children's Privacy

Rosetta PNO is designed for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

7. International Users

If you are accessing our services from outside the United States, please be aware that your information may be transferred to and processed in the United States, where data protection laws may differ from those in your country.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by posting the new policy on our website and updating the "Last Updated" date. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Refund Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2025

At Rosetta Project, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. This Refund Policy outlines the terms for refunds on donations and hardware pre-orders.

1. Rosetta PNO Software

Rosetta PNO is free and open-source software. No refunds are applicable as there is no purchase price.

2. Voluntary Donations

Donations to support the Rosetta Project are voluntary contributions and are generally non-refundable. However, if you made a donation in error or under exceptional circumstances, please contact us at support@rosetta.fun within 7 days, and we will review your request on a case-by-case basis.

3. Acoustica Digitalis Hardware Pre-Orders

Refundable Deposits

If you selected a refundable deposit option ($100 tier):

  • You may request a full refund at any time before your unit ships
  • Refunds will be processed within 14 business days
  • Once your unit has shipped, the deposit is no longer refundable

Non-Refundable Deposits

If you selected a non-refundable deposit option ($500 tier with 10% discount):

  • Your deposit is non-refundable under standard circumstances
  • Refunds may be granted in cases of manufacturing delays exceeding 12 months from the original estimated delivery date
  • If the product is significantly different from what was advertised, you may be eligible for a refund

Post-Delivery Returns

Once your Acoustica Digitalis unit has been delivered:

  • You have 30 days from the delivery date to request a return
  • The unit must be in like-new condition with all original packaging and accessories
  • You are responsible for return shipping costs unless the unit is defective
  • A restocking fee of 15% may apply to non-defective returns
  • Refunds will be processed within 14 business days of receiving the returned unit

4. Defective Products

If your Acoustica Digitalis unit arrives damaged or defective:

  • Contact us within 7 days of delivery with photos/video of the issue
  • We will provide a prepaid return label at no cost to you
  • You may choose a replacement unit or a full refund
  • No restocking fees apply to defective products

5. Warranty

Acoustica Digitalis hardware includes a 1-year limited warranty covering manufacturing defects. The warranty does not cover:

  • Normal wear and tear
  • Damage from misuse, accidents, or unauthorized modifications
  • Software issues (Rosetta PNO is maintained separately as open-source software)

6. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund or return:

  1. Email support@rosetta.fun with your order number and reason for the refund
  2. Include any supporting documentation (photos, videos, receipts)
  3. Our team will review your request within 3 business days
  4. If approved, refunds will be issued to the original payment method within 14 business days

7. Manufacturing Delays

Due to the custom nature of Acoustica Digitalis, delivery timelines are estimates. If your order is delayed beyond the estimated delivery window:

  • We will notify you via email with updated timelines
  • If delays exceed 6 months, you may request a full refund (even for non-refundable deposits)
  • If delays exceed 12 months, all customers are eligible for full refunds

8. Cancellations

You may cancel your pre-order at any time before your unit ships (subject to the refund tier you selected). Once production has begun on your specific unit, cancellations may not be possible, and you will be notified if this is the case.

Important: Refunds are subject to the payment processor's terms and may take 5-10 business days to appear on your statement after being issued by us.
