The Rosetta Method

Traditional music education creates a cognitive bottleneck: read symbol → translate → locate → play.

Rosetta uses falling light arrays aligned with keys to treat music as visual-spatial flow, bypassing conscious decoding and writing directly into procedural memory.

Leveraging Long-Term Potentiation and the ethology of play, users experience primal joy while mastering complex pieces.

Rosetta PNO

Cyber-Conservatory aesthetic: minimalist dark mode with high-contrast neon.
